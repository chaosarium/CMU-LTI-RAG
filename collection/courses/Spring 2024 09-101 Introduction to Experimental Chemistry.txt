<start course description for 09-101 Introduction to Experimental Chemistry>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This is a seven week chemistry laboratory course that is designed to introduce students to some basic laboratory skills, techniques, and equipment commonly used in experimental chemical investigations. The experiments will apply concepts in organic synthesis, quantitative analysis using visible spectrophotometry, kinetics, acid-base chemistry, thermochemistry, and transition metal coordination chemistry.  1 hr. lec., 3 hrs. lab.
