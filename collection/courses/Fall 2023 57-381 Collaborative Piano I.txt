<start course description for 57-381 Collaborative Piano I>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This class is the first in a series of hands-on courses which allow the student to accumulate experience accompanying in a professional venue. Students will be assigned to a vocal and/or instrumental studio and will have the opportunity to coach repertoire with a professional accompanist. Assignments may include playing for instrumental juries.
