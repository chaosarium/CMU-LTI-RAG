<start course description for 21-880 Stochastic Calculus>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This is a first Ph.D.-level course in stochastic calculus for continuous-time processes. It includes martingales and semi-martingales, Brownian motion, the Poisson process, representation of continuous martingales as time-changed Brownian motions, construction of the Ito integral, and Ito's formula.  (Two 80 minute lectures)
