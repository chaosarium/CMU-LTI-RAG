<start course description for 47-739 Topics in Consumer Behavior>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course is designed with three main goals:   1)    To help you understand what makes for good research in consumer behavior. 2)    To give you an overview of a selection of topics in social and cognitive psychology as they inform consumer behavior and decision-making. 3)    To expose you to a range of methodological techniques that might inspire novel research.
