<start course description for 85-480 Internship in Clinical Psychology>
Semester: Summer One/All 2024 (aka M24, M24 Mini 1 or M24 Full, Summer 2024 Mini 1)
Course Description: This course allows students to gain applied clinical experience in a mental health setting. Students will work alongside psychology professionals at designated field placements. This course is designed to help students apply and expand their knowledge of clinical psychology and to develop appropriate professional work standards. Students will spend the majority of their time (8 hours per week) in an applied clinical setting, with a one hour per week supervision meeting with Dr. Kasey Creswell. Students must be currently enrolled in 85-422 (Clinical Psychology: Science and Practice) or have already taken this course. Instructor permission is required. Please contact Dr. Kasey Creswell if you are interested in enrolling at kasey@andrew.cmu.edu.
</end course description for 85-480 Internship in Clinical Psychology>
