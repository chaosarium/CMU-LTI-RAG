<start course description for 80-501 Undergraduate Research in Philosophy>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course is for students pursuing a research project under the supervision of a faculty member in the Philosophy Department. Time commitments as well as specific requirements, expectations, and deliverables of the research experience must be worked out in advance between the student and the supervising faculty member. The units can vary depending on the scope of the project.
