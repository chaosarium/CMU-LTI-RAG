<start course description for 69-165 Cycling Core>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Indoor cycling classes are riding on a stationary bike while getting a great workout, experiencing several styles of training, and listening to music.  All are welcome--beginner to advanced--you set the workout pace to various intensities.  This course is for those participants who want to gain knowledge and experience of riding for endurance, speed work, race training, strength training, and/or visionary riding.    Each class will be formatted to take the rider to their levels of advancement--beginner to advanced--all doing the same workout.  Bikes are provided.  No prior bike experience is necessary.  No special footwear required--bike shoes are welcome--and tennis shoes at least are a must.    Come along for the ride of a lifetime while having fun and getting into shape.
</end course description for 69-165 Cycling Core>
