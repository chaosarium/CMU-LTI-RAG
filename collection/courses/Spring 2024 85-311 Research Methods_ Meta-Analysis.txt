<start course description for 85-311 Research Methods: Meta-Analysis>

Course Description: The scientific process is inherently cumulative: Scientific understanding moves forward by building on the theories, methods, and findings of individual scientistics. The broad aim of this course is to teach you a set of practical, modern tools for conducting psychological research that facilitate cumulative scientific progress.
