Course Description: The main purpose of this course is to provide students with a basic understanding of supervised and unsupervised machine learning models.   This course will face students with real examples and real-world data, as an increasing number of organizations nowadays collect data to support their decision-making process. Learning from data can enable us to better: evaluate sales decisions, make a medical diagnosis, monitor the reliability of IT systems, p market segmentation, improve the success of marketing campaigns, and much, much more.
