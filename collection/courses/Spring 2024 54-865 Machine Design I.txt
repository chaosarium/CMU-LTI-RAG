<start course description for 54-865 Machine Design I>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Required for all senior undergraduate and second-year graduate Technical Direction students.    In this course, concepts from Physics of Stage Machinery are applied to the specification, selection, design and assembly of real-world mechanical components for the realization of winches, turntables, wagons and lifts for theatrical use.    Drama majors only, or with instructor permission.
