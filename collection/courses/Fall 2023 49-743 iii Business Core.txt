<start course description for 49-743 iii Business Core>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course is focused on building a lattice of skills that will integrate the main business functions of Finance, Operations and Marketing and Strategy into a unified decision-making framework. The goal is for students to understand and appreciate the multi-dimensional reality of how business is conducted.  We will use a dual thread of theory and practice.  Students will be solving novel problems with tools in a team setting so as to validate their understanding of the business problem solving approaches.
</end course description for 49-743 iii Business Core>
