<start course description for 51-903 Design Theory & Practice>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course advances your understanding of how methods from diverse disciplinary domains can inform new kinds of research and practice. Spanning 15-weeks, including a 2-day intensive workshop, you will explore the intersections of theory, practice and praxis, to develop a holistic understanding of research as reflexive theoretical and practical process. Coursework includes the design of praxis methods to shape your own research. Topics include: Research Of Design; Research For Design; Research By Design; and, Design Praxis.
</end course description for 51-903 Design Theory & Practice>
