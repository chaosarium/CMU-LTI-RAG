<start course description for 51-341 How Things are Made>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course will provide a breadth of knowledge for current manufacturing, materials, and processes encountered in the industrial design field.  There will be an emphasis on actual production/manufacture methods and not rapid prototyping methods. The class will consist of various lectures, media, electronic tools, and on-site visits to enable an understanding of how mass production affects design and design decisions.  Industrial Design Juniors & Seniors or permission of the instructor.
</end course description for 51-341 How Things are Made>
