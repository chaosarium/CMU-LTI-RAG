<start course description for 82-709 Translation as a Profession III>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: We will prepare for the job market with the help of Mary Jayne McCullough, who owns the translation agency Global Wordsmiths in Pittsburgh. We will cover a number of important areas such as doing your own taxes, bookkeeping, budgeting, writing compelling resumes and cover letters, setting up your own website, and marketing your own business. In addition, we will continue to hear from guest speakers from the Pittsburgh area who are involved in the translation or language industry to get more advice from professionals
