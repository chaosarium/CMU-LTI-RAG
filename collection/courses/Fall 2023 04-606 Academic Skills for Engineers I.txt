Course Description: This course aims to expand students vocabulary and grammar and introduce them to a range of academic skills to support them in their MS programs. The skills will include academic writing, summarizing texts, preparing and delivering presentations, and research and seminar skills.  This course is offered on a pass/no pass basis.
