<start course description for 76-339 South Asian Cinemas: Bollywood & Beyond>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Topics vary by semester. Spring 2024: South Asian Cinemas: Bollywood & Beyond Spring 2024: South Asian Cinemas: Bollywood & Beyond  This course will go beyond popular Hindi Cinema (aka Bollywood) and examine the many film cultures of South Asia. It will provide a historical overview of the film industries in the region, including Hindi cinema, Bengali cinema, Telegu cinema, and Pakistani Cinema, and explore their aesthetic, social, and political implications. Additionally, the course will introduce students to a wide range of films and film genres from South Asia, including popular Bollywood films, women's film, regional/national cinema, arthouse/parallel cinema, and diasporic cinema.
