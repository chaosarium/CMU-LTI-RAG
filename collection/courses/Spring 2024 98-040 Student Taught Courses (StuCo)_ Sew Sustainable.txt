<start course description for 98-040 Student Taught Courses (StuCo): Sew Sustainable>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: The goal of this course is to teach students the sewing basics required for maintenance, repair, and alteration of clothes. Sewing is a valuable life skill that can enhance the fit and style of clothes, save you from a wardrobe malfunction, and save you money. Mending will help you reduce your environmental impact, by lowering waste and consumption. Hand sewing and machine sewing will be covered, as will darning techniques for holes of various sizes, shapes, and material. Each class will include an introduction to a new technique. Students will have time to practice that technique on a piece of scrap fabric, or a piece of clothing brought from home. Classes will also include the history and cultural context for the techniques being taught.
</end course description for 98-040 Student Taught Courses (StuCo): Sew Sustainable>
