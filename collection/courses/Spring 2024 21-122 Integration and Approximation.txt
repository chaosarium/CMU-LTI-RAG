<start course description for 21-122 Integration and Approximation>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Integration by trigonometric substitution and partial fractions; arclength; improper integrals; Simpson's and Trapezoidal Rules for numerical integration; separable differential equations, Newton's method, Euler's method, Taylor's Theorem including a discussion of the remainder, sequences, series, power series. Parametric curves, polar coordinates, vectors, dot product. (Three 50 minute lectures, two 50 minute recitations)
</end course description for 21-122 Integration and Approximation>
