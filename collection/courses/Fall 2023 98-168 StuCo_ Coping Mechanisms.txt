<start course description for 98-168 StuCo: Coping Mechanisms>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: The course will be mainly discussion based. It will cover how students can develop coping strategies and mechanisms that can help and aid them in their life (professional and personal life), some psychology topics will be discussed along with those strategies and studies about those specific topics will be presented to students. Activities will be done in classes such as: Organizational Games, Art Sessions, and Game Night to let students benefit while learning about the topics and learn how certain coping mechanism topics are related to those activities.
</end course description for 98-168 StuCo: Coping Mechanisms>
