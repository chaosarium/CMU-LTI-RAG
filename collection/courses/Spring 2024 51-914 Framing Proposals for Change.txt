<start course description for 51-914 Framing Proposals for Change>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Introduction of techniques to support doctoral students in framing research projects geared toward steering sustainable change in the world. Spanning 7 weeks, this course helps to frame research contexts, define research questions, plan methodological approaches, and speculate upon outcomes and their impacts. Coursework includes the development of a PhD Research Proposal.
