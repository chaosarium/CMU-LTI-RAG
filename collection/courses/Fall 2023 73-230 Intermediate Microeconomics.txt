<start course description for 73-230 Intermediate Microeconomics>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course builds on the Principles of Economics course. It focuses on the following topics: theory of the consumer, theory of the producer, perfectly competitive market equilibrium, imperfect competition, and market failures due to asymmetric information such as adverse selection and moral hazard.  (Lecture, 3 hours; Recitation, 1 hour).
