<start course description for 57-382 Collaborative Piano II>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: These are a series of courses which allow the student the opportunity to accumulate experience in a professional setting with students and faculty.  The pianist will be assigned to a voice studio(s) and play for students and their professional mentors, supervised by a Collaborative Piano faculty member.  Assignments may include playing voice juries.  This course is open to Music Majors and Non-Music Majors, based upon a mandatory audition prior to approval.
</end course description for 57-382 Collaborative Piano II>
