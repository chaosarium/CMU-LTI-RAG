<start course description for 54-319 Cabaret>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: The Art of Cabaret:   Explores the use of Stories and Song to communicate life experiences within an intimate setting, breaking down the invisible fourth wall for honest communication.    The course includes a section on the use of the microphone for singers.   This Study produces two Cabarets containing Material on a chosen Theme to provide hands-on Song Expression in a public forum.
