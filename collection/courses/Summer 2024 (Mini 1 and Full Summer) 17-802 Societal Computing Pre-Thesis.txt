<start course description for 17-802 Societal Computing Pre-Thesis>
Semester: Summer One/All 2024 (aka M24, M24 Mini 1 or M24 Full, Summer 2024 Mini 1)
Course Description: Note: This course previously offered as 08-802. This course is provided by each Societal Computing advisor to coach the student in preparing, presenting, and passing a thesis proposal.  The proposal will generally occur in the semester where this course is taken.  If it is not, then, at the advisor's discretion, an incomplete may be granted.  In that case, in accordance with university policy, the course must be completed (and the thesis passed) no later than the last day of the following semester, or the default grade will be awarded.
</end course description for 17-802 Societal Computing Pre-Thesis>
