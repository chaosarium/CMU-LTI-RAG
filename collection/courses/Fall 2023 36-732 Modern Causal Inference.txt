<start course description for 36-732 Modern Causal Inference>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course will provide an in-depth look at modern causal inference. Topics will include: optimal treatment regimes, mediation, principal stratification, stochastic interventions, accounting for complex confounding and exposures, and methods for efficient nonparametric estimation. Some background in mathematical statistics is advised.
