Course Description: In this required capstone project MITS students are expected to meet with their assigned teams as often as needed to finalize project outcomes, while providing timely updates to the project advisor. Registration in the MITS Capstone Project course is limited to those students who have been accepted into the MITS program. The capstone project provides students with a laboratory for direct application of concepts learned in core courses.
