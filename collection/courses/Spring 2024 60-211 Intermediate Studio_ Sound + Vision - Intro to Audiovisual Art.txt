<start course description for 60-211 Intermediate Studio: Sound + Vision - Intro to Audiovisual Art>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Sound + Vision is an introduction to the analysis and making of visual music, music video, expanded cinema and sound design for moving image media.   Through presentations, screenings, demos and workshops students will study the influences of sound on the perception of space and time, and the impact audiovision has on our experience of moving image media. Working on their own and in small groups, students will complete short sound experiments and develop a variety of audiovisual projects.
</end course description for 60-211 Intermediate Studio: Sound + Vision - Intro to Audiovisual Art>
