<start course description for 57-591 Dalcroze Pedagogy/Practice Teaching>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course gives hands-on experience in applying Dalcroze principles in teaching situations.  It is designed for students interested in learning about the teaching of Eurhythmics, general Music Education, and for those considering the Dalcroze Certificate. The class will meet in a three week rotation of two Thursday evenings followed by a Saturday morning with the Preparatory School children's classes.
