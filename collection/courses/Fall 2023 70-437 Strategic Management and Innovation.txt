<start course description for 70-437 Strategic Management and Innovation>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course focuses on how organizations deliver value in changing business environments, using analytical techniques to evaluate business situations and develop strategies that improve organizational performance. By focusing on the organizational perspective, you will develop practical skills in evaluating and designing ways for firms to achieve key strategic outcomes like growth and innovation, which determine the firm's chances of success in dynamic environments. This course is particularly useful if you are interested in careers such as consulting, business development, investment banking, private equity, entrepreneurship, or simply want a better understanding of how their firm can develop a more innovative outlook.
