<start course description for 21-590 Practicum in Mathematical Sciences>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Students in this course gain experience with the application of mathematical models to business and/or industrial problems during an internship.  The internship is set up by the student in consultation with a faculty member.  The students must also have a mentor at the firm providing the internship, who together with the faculty member develops a description of the goals of the internship.  The internship must include the opportunity to learn about problems which have mathematical content.
</end course description for 21-590 Practicum in Mathematical Sciences>
