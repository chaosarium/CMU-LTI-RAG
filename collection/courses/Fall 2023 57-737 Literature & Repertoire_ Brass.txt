<start course description for 57-737 Literature & Repertoire: Brass>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course deals with literature and repertoire for orchestral instruments.  There are multiple sections organized by instrument categories or specific instruments, as follows:  Brass, Cello, Double Bass, Flute, Harp, Opera, Organ, Percussion, Trombone, Solo Trumpet, Tuba, Viola, Violin, and Woodwind. Piano Literature and Repertoire is listed as 57-881 through 57-884.
