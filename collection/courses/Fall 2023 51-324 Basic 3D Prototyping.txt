<start course description for 51-324 Basic 3D Prototyping>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: A half-semester laboratory mini-course introducing a range of materials, methods, and workshop techniques by which designers prototype designs in three dimensions. Basic competence in shop techniques is established by bringing to realization a series of simple artifacts. Studio and model shop tools are required; lab fee.  Instructor permission required for non-Design majors.
</end course description for 51-324 Basic 3D Prototyping>
