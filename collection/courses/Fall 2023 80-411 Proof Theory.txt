<start course description for 80-411 Proof Theory>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: An introduction to the general study of deductive systems and their properties. Topics include the natural deduction and sequent calculi; cut-elimination and normalization theorems; metamathematical properties of first-order logic and theories of arithmetic; and conservation theorems.
