<start course description for 49-705 It Depends: An Inquiry into Innovative Thinking>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This micro explores how mindset and ways of seeing the world can influence our ability to bring innovative thinking to our sometimes seemingly ambiguous work. Through readings, exercises, discussions, and debates we explore the traits and lateral thinking that lead to fearless, curious, explorative work and how a true growth mindset can make a difference.
