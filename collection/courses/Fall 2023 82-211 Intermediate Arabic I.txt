<start course description for 82-211 Intermediate Arabic I>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course builds on Elementary Arabic II to continue building students' communicative competence at the Intermediate Low-Mid level in Modern Standard Arabic in all four language skills (listening, reading, speaking, and writing) following a proficiency-oriented teaching approach. The course also continues to 1) integrate a spoken dialect to enrich students' background in oral communication; and 2) educate students about to various aspects of Arab culture through written and audio-visual materials.
</end course description for 82-211 Intermediate Arabic I>
