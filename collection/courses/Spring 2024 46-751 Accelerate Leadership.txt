Course Description: MSPM students are required to participate in leadership training through the Tepper School Accelerate program. The Accelerate Leadership Center offers one-to-one coaching, a series of leadership workshops and Leadership Development Certification for students to create action plans that advance their personal and professional leadership goals. The Accelerate Leadership Center offers a premier leadership development experience and enhances high-performance analytical skills with essential leadership and communication behaviors.
