<start course description for 84-705 CMIST Graduate Internship>
Semester: Summer One/All 2024 (aka M24, M24 Mini 1 or M24 Full, Summer 2024 Mini 1)
Course Description: CMIST graduate students are required to complete a summer internship in a related field between the first and second year of the program.  The internship will allow students to synthesize the program's studies in the context of practical and hands-on experiential learning opportunities.  CMIST has dedicated staff to support students in all aspects of the internship process.
