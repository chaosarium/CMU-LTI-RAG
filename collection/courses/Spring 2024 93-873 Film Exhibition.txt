Course Description: This course will focus on understanding how the Film Exhibition Industry is key to conceptualize, create and launch content. The economic rationale is the driver behind the films, which maintain industry profitability and hence-forth guarantee the sustainability of the sector over time. At the end of the course, students will be able to understand and identify the critical factors in the success of film exhibition management in order to make the best decisions based on the economic rationale.
