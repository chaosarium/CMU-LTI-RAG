<start course description for 33-445 Advanced Quantum Physics I>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Mathematics of quantum theory, linear algebra and Hilbert spaces; review of classical mechanics; problems with classical mechanics; postulates of quantum theory; one dimensional applications; the harmonic oscillator; uncertainty relations; systems with N degrees of freedom, multi-particle states, identical particles; approximation methods. This course has a co-requisite of 33-331.
