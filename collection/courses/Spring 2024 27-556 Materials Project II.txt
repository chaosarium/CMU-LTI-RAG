<start course description for 27-556 Materials Project II>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Second semester of Materials Project.  This course is designed to give experience in individualized research under the guidance of a faculty member.  The topic is selected by mutual agreement, and will give the student a chance to study the literature, design experiments, interpret the results and present the conclusions orally and in writing. Faculty advisor selection must be in consultation with the MSE Undergraduate Academic Advisor.
