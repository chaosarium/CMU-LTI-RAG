<start course description for 76-869 Professionalization for LCS>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course is strongly recommended for all LCS PHDs. In this course you will be introduced to the variety of things you will need when you enter the job market. We'll discuss how the job market is organized, how the job market is changing, how to choose a subfield within English/Cultural Studies, conferences, choosing a dissertation topic, and more.
