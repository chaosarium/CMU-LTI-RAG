<start course description for 06-323 Heat and Mass Transfer>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course presents the fundamentals of heat and mass transfer, including steady-state and transient heat conduction and molecular diffusion, convection of heat and mass, and thermal radiation, with application to heat and mass transfer processes. Development of dimensionless quantities for engineering analysis is emphasized.
