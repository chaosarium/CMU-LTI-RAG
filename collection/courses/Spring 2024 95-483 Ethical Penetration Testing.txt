<start course description for 95-483 Ethical Penetration Testing>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This is an undergraduate course.  Graduate students looking to take this course should register for 95-883.  This course will introduce students to professional penetration testing by teaching offensive tactics along with the appropriate methodologies and responsibilities it takes to ethically attack systems. The majority of time will be spent in hands-on labs performing reconnaissance, discovering vulnerabilities, developing exploits, and carefully penetrating targets.
