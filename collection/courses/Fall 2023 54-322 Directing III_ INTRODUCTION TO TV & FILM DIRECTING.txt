<start course description for 54-322 Directing III: INTRODUCTION TO TV & FILM DIRECTING>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: An intensive introduction to directing with a camera in narrative film and episodic television for third year undergraduate directing and dramaturgy students.  Students learn how to prepare for a camera shoot including breaking down a script, camera terminology, shot types and what can be accomplished, how to make a shot lists, visual style and how to express it. Students learn shot progression in the scene and point-of-view. This class will also include working with actors where we use a camera to film them under strict COVID guidelines.
</end course description for 54-322 Directing III: INTRODUCTION TO TV & FILM DIRECTING>
