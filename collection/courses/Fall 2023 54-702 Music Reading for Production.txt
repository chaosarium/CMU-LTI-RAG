<start course description for 54-702 Music Reading for Production>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This class gives the basics of music theory, musical terminology and score reading.  Students focus on the difference in various musical scores, ie. piano/vocal, full, hand written scores.  Students are guided in classroom listening which a wide variety of music including, opera, musical theatre, ballet, and choral/orchestra works.
