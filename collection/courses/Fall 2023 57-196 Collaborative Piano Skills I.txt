<start course description for 57-196 Collaborative Piano Skills I>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: A required course for first year piano majors. The skills include sightreading, basic keyboard harmony, transposition, and improvised accompaniments for popular or musical theater songs from either a piano reduction or a lead sheet. The students participate in collaborative situations such as juries, recitals, and class presentations. The presentations are critiqued by the instructor and by other students.
</end course description for 57-196 Collaborative Piano Skills I>
