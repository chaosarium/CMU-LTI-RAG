<start course description for 54-934 Costume Crafts: Fabric Modification>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course is intended to introduce the student to processes of fabric modification that utilize techniques beyond painting and dyeing.  Students will be encouraged to investigate the world of garment decoration and to actively explore a wide variety of processes and materials ranging from old world needle arts to modern crafts which utilize long established techniques in contemporary context.
