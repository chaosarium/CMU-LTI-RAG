<start course description for 54-214 Singing for Actors II>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: The students have a class voice experience which includes a physical and vocal warm-up and discussion and practice of healthy singing technique.  There is group and individual rehearsal of potential audition and performance material. Toward the end of the term, there are weekly opportunities to perform in public, thus preparing for auditions.
