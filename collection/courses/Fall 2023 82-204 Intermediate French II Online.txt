<start course description for 82-204 Intermediate French II Online>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: An integrated approach to the study of the French language and culture by  means of grammar review, literary and cultural readings and analysis, and  intensive practice in written and spoken French. This course explores  definitions of culture and analyzes the dynamic role of language in culture  and culture in language, with an aim to foster cross-cultural awareness and  self-realization while developing proficiency in French.    A continuation of Intermediate French 1, with an emphasis on  francophone cultures outside of France. There is a required weekly class  meeting for training and for group activities, plus individual weekly meetings with a tutor or the instructor for conversation and practice.
</end course description for 82-204 Intermediate French II Online>
