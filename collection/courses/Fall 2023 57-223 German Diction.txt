<start course description for 57-223 German Diction>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: In-depth study of German diction - development of legato vocal style in German through the analysis of grammatical usage, word construction, vowel colorization and consonant articulation. Included are in-class German diction evaluations, peer assessment, and emphasis on competency in using the International Phonetic Alphabet.
