<start course description for 57-355 Secondary Guided Teaching>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course enables students to apply instructional strategies in local secondary school music classes. School visits provide opportunities to work with band, choral, & orchestral ensembles and general music classes. Seminar discussions with the cooperating teachers familiarize students with both school-wide and classroom management issues that affect teaching, learning, motivation, and the administration of music programs.
</end course description for 57-355 Secondary Guided Teaching>
