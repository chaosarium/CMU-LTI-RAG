<start course description for 98-369 Student Taught Courses (StuCo): Brooklyn Nine-Nine>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Brooklyn Nine-Nine is a cop comedy that first aired in 2013 and is continuing to this day. This Emmy, Golden Globe, Critics Choice, and GLAAD Media award-winning show centers around Jake Peralta and the rest of the 99th precinct of the NYPD. Part of Brooklyn Nine-Nines success is due to their skill at balancing a comedic atmosphere while also addressing and discussing current social issues. In this class, you will learn about a social issue each week and then watch an episode that addresses said social issue. Anyone is free to join the noicest StuCo at CMU - no prior knowledge is required!
</end course description for 98-369 Student Taught Courses (StuCo): Brooklyn Nine-Nine>
