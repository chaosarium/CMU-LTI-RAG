Course Description: This course is a broad introduction to teaching techniques and DEI issues in the context of computer science education.  It is targeted to first-time undergraduate and graduate SCS TAs, with the goal of providing instruction, hands-on practice, and feedback to TAs to improve their instructional proficiency.
