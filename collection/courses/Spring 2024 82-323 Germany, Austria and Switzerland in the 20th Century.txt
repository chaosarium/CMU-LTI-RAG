<start course description for 82-323 Germany, Austria and Switzerland in the 20th Century>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course advances proficiency in communicative and grammatical skills in the German language and knowledge of German-speaking cultures through the study of important events, trends, and people of the twentieth century in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. Examples will be drawn from literature, newspapers, television, film and other sources. Students will be expected to complete assignments that demonstrate the ability to express critical judgments in both written and oral form, documented through readings and personal research. The course includes a review of the most troublesome points of German grammar.
</end course description for 82-323 Germany, Austria and Switzerland in the 20th Century>
