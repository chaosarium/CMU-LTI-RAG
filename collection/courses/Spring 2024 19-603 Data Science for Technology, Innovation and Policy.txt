<start course description for 19-603 Data Science for Technology, Innovation and Policy>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Students will learn how to use R to collect, organize, and analyze data in technology, innovation, and policy-related domains. The focus will be on the practical issues faced when conducting data analyses, correctly implementing and interpreting statistical models, and summarizing results for clients and research purposes.
