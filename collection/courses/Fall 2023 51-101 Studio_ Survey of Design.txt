<start course description for 51-101 Studio: Survey of Design>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Students will conduct activities that will help them notice design in the world, investigate how it works, and describe their thinking about design, through photography, video capture, sketching, note-taking and modeling. They will work through projects in various ways as a means of 'testing-out' and reflecting on command design approaches. This course is for undergraduate design majors only.
</end course description for 51-101 Studio: Survey of Design>
