<start course description for 51-262 Design Center: Communication and Digital Design Fundamentals>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: A one-semester course that introduces non-majors to the field of communication design. Through studio projects, lectures, and demonstrations, students become familiar with the visual and verbal language of communication designers, the design process, and the communicative value of word and image. Macintosh proficiency required.
