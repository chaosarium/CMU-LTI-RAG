<start course description for 05-774 Design Perspectives in HCI>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: One of a series of four, seminar style mini-courses, to expose our PhD students to the breadth of classic and cutting edge research in four distinct traditions in HCII--computer science, cognitive science, social science, and design. Although no project is required for this course, there will be significant reading and writing.
