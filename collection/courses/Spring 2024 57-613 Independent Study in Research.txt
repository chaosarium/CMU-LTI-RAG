<start course description for 57-613 Independent Study in Research>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Students undertake a critical examination of some aspects of music on an independent basis under the supervision of a full-time faculty member. They choose their topic and contract with the Project Director (faculty sponsor) as to when and how the project will be completed. Open to upperclassmen.
