<start course description for 88-499 Advanced Undergraduate Research>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Students conduct research at an advanced level under the supervision of a Social & Decision Sciences faculty member. Students who wish to engage in advanced research should seek out a faculty member whose interests are appropriate to the research. Students must also complete an "Independent Study/Research for Credit" form, available from the SDS Coordinator of Student Programs in Porter 208A.  Prerequisite: Permission of a faculty sponsor.
