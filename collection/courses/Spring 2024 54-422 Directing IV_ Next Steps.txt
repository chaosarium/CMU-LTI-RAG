<start course description for 54-422 Directing IV: Next Steps>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: An exploration of the "next steps" in your Directing training and in the trajectory of your Directing career. The mastery of the art and craft of directing is a lifelong process.  Through discussions, readings, exercises, projects and explorations, this course is intended to give the aspiring director added, supplementary or not-yet-addressed tools to add to their existing foundations and their director's toolbox.
