<start course description for 49-760 Foundations of Software Management>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Students apply fundamental methods, models, and frameworks to assess real software companies from a variety of perspectives - marketing, strategy, finance, operations - to understand how businesses organize and make decisions.  Working individually and in groups, students develop skills for managing teams and employee performance.  Students practice personal leadership. Note: enrollment into this course is reserved for incoming Master of Science in Software Management Students.
