<start course description for 45-760 Operations Management>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course is for MBA students only.  This course covers fundamental optimization tools for quantitative analysis in the management sciences. The central topics of study are linear integer and nonlinear programming. Special emphasis is placed on linear programming particularly on modeling business applications and on sensitivity analysis. The course follows a practical spreadsheet-based approach to provide hands-on experience with software such as Excel Solver.
