<start course description for 45-965 Service Management:  Go-to-Market Strataegy and Operations>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: As the world's major economies have matured, they have become dominated by service providing organizations. These service focused businesses respond to the requirements of customers to satisfy some needs and leave certain experiences in their minds. This course addresses the strategic and operational aspects of managing service systems, with emphasis on specific issues from certain sectors (such as health care and consulting).  The focus is:  how can firms deliver excellend service while achieving business success?  this means we have to understand and influence customers, anticipate actions by competitors and design th firm's internal mechanisms.
</end course description for 45-965 Service Management:  Go-to-Market Strataegy and Operations>
