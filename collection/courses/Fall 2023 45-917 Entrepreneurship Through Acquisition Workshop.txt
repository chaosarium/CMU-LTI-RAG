<start course description for 45-917 Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition Workshop>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Entrepreneurship through Acquisition Workshop is reserved for students who have a clear and immediate interest in initiating a business acquisition search during the remaining months of their MBA program. This course will guide the students step by step through the acquisition process including:  Choosing the optimal funding source, Target industry identification, Setting up to search, Private Placement Memorandum, Target company list   All students who desire to enroll in this course must complete 45-817 or receive faculty approval prior to enrollment.
</end course description for 45-917 Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition Workshop>
