<start course description for 54-330 Introduction to Stage Management>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course is intended to provide students an opening to the knowledge and skills of the professional stage manager.  It will also illuminate the qualities of a good stage manager specific to personality and human interaction.  Within this course we will examine the role of the stage manager throughout the full scope of creating a production, including preparatory work, rehearsal period, technical rehearsal, performance and closing.
</end course description for 54-330 Introduction to Stage Management>
