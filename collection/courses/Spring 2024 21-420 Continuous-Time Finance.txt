<start course description for 21-420 Continuous-Time Finance>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course begins with Brownian motion, stochastic integration,and Ito's formula from stochastic calculus.  This theory is used to develop the Black-Scholes option pricing formula and the Black-Scholes partial differential equation.  Additional topics may include models of credit risk, simulation, and expected utility maximization.  (Three 50 minute lectures)
