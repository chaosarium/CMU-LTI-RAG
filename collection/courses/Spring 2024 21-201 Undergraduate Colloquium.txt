Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: The purpose of this course is to introduce math majors to the different degree programs in Mathematical Sciences, and to inform math majors about relevant topics such as advising, math courses, graduate schools, and typical career paths in the mathematical sciences.  The Career and Professional Development Center will present modules on professional communication, developing interview and networking skills, and preparing for career fairs.
