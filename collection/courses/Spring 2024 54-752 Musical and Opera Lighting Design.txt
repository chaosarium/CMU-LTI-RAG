<start course description for 54-752 Musical and Opera Lighting Design>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Through hands-on lab exercises and preparation of full lighting design plots, students will study lighting design for Musical Theater and Opera. The class will learn to visually analyze the emotional content of music, explore the various forms of musical performance, learn how to develop a design process, create focus in a large-scale space, and strategies for implementing a design.
</end course description for 54-752 Musical and Opera Lighting Design>
