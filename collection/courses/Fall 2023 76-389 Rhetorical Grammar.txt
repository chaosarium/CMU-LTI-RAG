<start course description for 76-389 Rhetorical Grammar>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This is a course in fundamental grammatical structures of English and how these structures fit into the writer's toolkit. This means you will learn a lot about English-language grammar in this course en route to understanding a lot about English language writing. This course is designed for MA students in professional writing and undergraduates who want to improve their grammar, their writing, and their depth of understanding of how improvement in grammar impacts improvement in writing.
</end course description for 76-389 Rhetorical Grammar>
