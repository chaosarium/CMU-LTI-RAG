<start course description for 49-757 Solution Strategy & Marketing>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course provides students with an understanding  of commercializing  a product in the dynamic high-tech industry.  Students apply concepts of effective strategy for effective product delivery.  This will include understanding the drivers for a strategic roadmap, creating scenarios for alternatives, market and technology analysis and establishing metrics for business success.  Students define a strategy and roadmap for delivering a minimum viable product that addresses the business or social problem they intend to improve.
</end course description for 49-757 Solution Strategy & Marketing>
