<start course description for 57-308 Bagpipe Advanced History>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course is an in-depth study of the origins of the bagpipe, including the oral tradition, the Hereditary Pipers and their teachings, piping in the military, prominent teachers, and a study of the Tree of Piping dating from MacCrimmon to the present day.
