<start course description for 54-453 Production Management Workshop>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Investigates the organization, planning and interpersonal skills required to successfully manage a live theatrical production. Course is discussion based on 1) participants experience in laboratory productions in the School of Drama, 2) current practical examples of experiences of professional production managers, and 3) contemporary management texts.  Topics covered include: Budgeting, Scheduling, Communication, and Project Management. Permission of instructor required.
