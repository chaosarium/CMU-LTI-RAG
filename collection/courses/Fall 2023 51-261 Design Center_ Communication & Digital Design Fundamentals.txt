<start course description for 51-261 Design Center: Communication & Digital Design Fundamentals>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: A one-semester course that introduces non-majors to the field of communication design. Through studio projects, lectures, and demonstrations, students become familiar with the visual and verbal language of communication designers, the design process, and the communicative value of word and image. Macintosh proficiency required.  This course (or 51262) is required for Design minors.     Section W - Qatar campus only
