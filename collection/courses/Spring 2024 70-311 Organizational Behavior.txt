<start course description for 70-311 Organizational Behavior>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: As you pursue your careers, you will inevitably depend on people to accomplish organizational goals; you will need to work for bosses, work with colleagues, and manage employees.  Your ability to do well depends on having an understanding of the human side of management. This course introduces insights and tools from the behavioral and social sciences that will improve your ability to think and act strategically in these situations.
