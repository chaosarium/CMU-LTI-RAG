<start course description for 85-482 Internship in Psychology>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: The Internship in Psychology is designed to enable students to gain experience in professional settings related to their studies in Psychology and earn credit for the intellectual work involved.  It is the students responsibility to locate an internship site and on-site supervisor, as well as to identify a CMU faculty sponsor.
