<start course description for 54-363 Dramaturgy 4: Devising>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Dramaturgy students only. Others by permission. In this class, students will: - gain an introduction to the history and practice of devised & documentary theatre - explore the dramaturg's role in devising theatre - investigate various methods for creating devised & documentary theatre - learn techniques for developing theatre from source material - create original devised pieces in collaboration with other students
