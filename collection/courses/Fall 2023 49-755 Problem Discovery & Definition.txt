<start course description for 49-755 Problem Discovery & Definition>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Students identify and describe meaningful business or social problem that can be improved in part by software technologies. Students apply a variety of techniques to elicit and model customer requirements, perform an analysis of competing products, and write a compelling vision for a creative product or service solution. Students create a product description document that captures solution alternatives for the business or social problem they are solving.
</end course description for 49-755 Problem Discovery & Definition>
