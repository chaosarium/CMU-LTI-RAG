<start course description for 90-770 Econometrics for Policy>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course will prepare the student to: (1) model social science problems and test them statistically and (2) begin understanding in-house reports and journal articles that use econometrics in order to make informed decisions. Students will accomplish these goals by learning how to tie economic theory to modeling and how statistical modeling works.  Students will discover ways to overcome some of the common problems with using regression analysis.
</end course description for 90-770 Econometrics for Policy>
