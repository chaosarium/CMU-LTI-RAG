<start course description for 57-152 Harmony I>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course deals with common-practice harmony. It includes triads and their inversions, tonality and modality, non-harmonic tones, cadences, and the basic concepts of modulation. Section assignment is determined by a placement test.
