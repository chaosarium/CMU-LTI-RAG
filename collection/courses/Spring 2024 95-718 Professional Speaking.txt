<start course description for 95-718 Professional Speaking>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Professional Speaking provides practical instruction for preparing and delivering professional presentations and communicating in professional situations. Activities and assignments include: developing targeted, strategic messages; structuring content; creating cohesive and meaningful visuals; exploring new technologies; working collaboratively in a group presentation; and, speaking extemporaneously. Students engage specific audiences using a communication style (both verbal and nonverbal) suitable for workplace environments. Overall, the course helps students develop confidence and apply effective techniques when speaking in a public setting.
</end course description for 95-718 Professional Speaking>
