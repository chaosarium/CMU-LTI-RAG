Course Description: 24-200  Machine Shop Practices    Fall and Spring Semesters, 1 units, 6 week mini course    This course familiarizes students with the operation and safety of machine tools. Prerequisite:  Undergraduate Mechanical Engineering standing    2 hours lab    Machine Shop Practices should be completed prior to Design II 24-441.
