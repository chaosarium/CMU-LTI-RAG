<start course description for 48-104 Shop Skills>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course will introduce basic material assembly methods, and the use of shop machinery, hand and power tools. It prepares students to participate in a wide range of subsequent building and fabrication projects. We will aim to build confidence and safe work habits while demystifying the interactions between tools and physical materials.
