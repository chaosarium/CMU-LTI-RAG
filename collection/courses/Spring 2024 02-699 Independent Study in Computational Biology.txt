<start course description for 02-699 Independent Study in Computational Biology>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: The student will, under the individual guidance of a faculty member, read and digest process papers or a textbook in an advanced area of computational biology not offered by an existing course at Carnegie Mellon.  The student will demonstrate their mastery of the material by a combination of one or more of the following: oral discussions with the faculty member; exercises set by the faculty member accompanying the readings; and a written summary synthesizing the material that the student learned. Permission required.
</end course description for 02-699 Independent Study in Computational Biology>
