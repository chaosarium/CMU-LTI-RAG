<start course description for 57-274 Piano Pedagogy II>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Beyond the beginning years: this course covers piano pedagogy of intermediate and early advanced level students. Topics include "What is a good piece?" Standard literature and technical development repertoire lists will be studied. The business of piano teaching and the instruction of college keyboard skills for non-piano majors will be discussed.
