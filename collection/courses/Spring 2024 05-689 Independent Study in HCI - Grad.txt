Course Description: With the permission of the professor, students may engage in independent project work on any number of innovative research projects sponsored by faculty. Students must complete an Independent Study Proposal, negotiate the number of units to be earned, submit a contract, and present a tangible deliverable.  The Program Advisor's signature is required for HCI graduate-level Independent Study courses.
