<start course description for 70-492 Investment Analysis>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Investment analysis provides you the concepts and tools used to analyze publicly traded securities, and you will learn how to these tools to real world situations. The course is organized as a tour of the different kinds of securities used in the financial markets. You will analyze how security prices are determined, the relations between the prices of different securities, their risks and returns, and how to choose a portfolio or strategy from different asset classes.
