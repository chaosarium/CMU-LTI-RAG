<start course description for 57-221 Italian Diction>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: A study of the fundamentals of Italian diction and development of legato vocal style through the analysis of grammatical usage, word construction, vowel colorization, and consonant articulation. Included are in-class performance evaluations, listening assignments, critiques, and private coachings.
</end course description for 57-221 Italian Diction>
