<start course description for 51-972 Coda II>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Coda provides critical space for divergent research themes to coalesce into a clear and conclusive body of research. You will assemble your dissertation ready for May of the final semester. Dissertations can be entirely written (60-80K words), or a body of written and practical work (40-60K words, by negotiation). Your final body of work must demonstrate an original contribution  of knowledge which expands understanding of transition design.
