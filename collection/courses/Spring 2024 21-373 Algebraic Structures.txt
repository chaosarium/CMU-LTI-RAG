<start course description for 21-373 Algebraic Structures>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Groups: Homomorphisms. Subgroups, cosets, Lagrange's theorem. Conjugation. Normal subgroups, quotient groups, first isomorphism theorem. Group actions, Cauchy's Theorem. Dihedral and alternating groups. The second and third isomorphism theorems. Rings:  Subrings, ideals, quotient rings, first isomorphism theorem. Polynomial rings. Prime and maximal ideals, prime and irreducible elements. PIDs and UFDs. Noetherian domains. Gauss' lemma. Eisenstein criterion. Fields:  Field of fractions of an integral domain. Finite fields. Applications to coding theory, cryptography, number theory. (Three 50 minute lectures)
</end course description for 21-373 Algebraic Structures>
