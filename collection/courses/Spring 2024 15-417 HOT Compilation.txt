<start course description for 15-417 HOT Compilation>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: The course covers the implementation of compilers for higher-order, typed languages such as ML and Haskell, and gives an introduction to type-preserving compilation.  Topics covered include type inference, elaboration, CPS conversion, closure conversion, garbage collection, phase splitting, and typed assembly language.
