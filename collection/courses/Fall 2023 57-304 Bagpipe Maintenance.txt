<start course description for 57-304 Bagpipe Maintenance>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: All aspects of bagpipe maintenance are covered in this course, from basic hemping and tying in bags to reeds set-up and manipulation.  The course includes study of all types of reeds, cane and synthetic, as well as drone and chanter, and recognition of pipemakers' patterns and distinctive hallmarks.
