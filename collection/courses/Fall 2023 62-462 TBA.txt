Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Build on what you learned in Flame 1. Continue to develop hand and glass control, learn to effectively multitask, and develop a greater confidence with a variety of flameworking techniques. You'll discover new skills such as working with Borosilicate tubing and color application while focusing on personal projects. Both group and one-on-one demonstrations will be provided as students work independently. Flame 1, or 24 hours of flameworking experience and instructor permission required. Class tuition includes 2 hours of open-studio time to be used during the 8-week course period. This will help you become familiar with the studio itself and learn the process of studio rental beyond class hours. Registration can only be done on or after your scheduled registration day. Please email Stefanie McGowan (stefanim@andrew.cmu.edu) for more information and to register. Registration is first come, first served. Course fee is $190. Not eligible for PCHE Cross Registration. Class is held at Pittsburgh Glass Center.
