<start course description for 57-927 Advanced Seminar in Film Musicology>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: This course has been designed primarily for advanced students wishing to apply to film scores analytical methodologies pertaining to historical musicology, cultural studies, and genetic criticism. The films screened and the music analyzed in this course follow at first the historical development of cinema. Then, the syllabus focuses on the film music of Ennio Morricone in honor of his 90th birthday and on final presentations of film soundtracks selected by the students.  Prerequisites include some knowledge of music history, theory, practice, or the instructor's permission.
</end course description for 57-927 Advanced Seminar in Film Musicology>
