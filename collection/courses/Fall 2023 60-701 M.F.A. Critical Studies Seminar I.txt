<start course description for 60-701 M.F.A. Critical Studies Seminar I>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: The two terms of MFA Critical Studies Seminar apply art history, theory, and criticism to nurture dialogue and develop a common language within each MFA cohort. Investigating artmaking beyond its material specifications and technical requirements, this course creates a decentered matrix through which MFA Candidates can contextualize their practice.
