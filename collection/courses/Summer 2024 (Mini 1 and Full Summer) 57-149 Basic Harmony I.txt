<start course description for 57-149 Basic Harmony I>
Semester: Summer One/All 2024 (aka M24, M24 Mini 1 or M24 Full, Summer 2024 Mini 1)
Course Description: This course deals with common-practice harmony. It includes triads and their inversions, tonality and modality, non-harmonic tones, cadences, and the basic concepts of modulation.  Section assignment is determined by a placement test.  It includes work on fundamentals for inexperienced students.
