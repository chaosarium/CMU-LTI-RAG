<start course description for 24-221 Thermodynamics>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Temperature and thermometry; equations of state for fluids and solids; work, heat, and the first law; internal energy, enthalpy, and specific heats; energy equations for flow; change of phase; the second law, reversibility, absolute temperature, and entropy; combined first and second laws; availability; power and refrigeration cycles. Applications to a wide range of processes and devices. 3 hrs. lec., 1 hour recitation
</end course description for 24-221 Thermodynamics>
