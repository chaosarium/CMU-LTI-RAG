<start course description for 70-409 Innovation and Entrepreneurship in Emerging Markets>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course focuses on the role that innovation-driven entrepreneurship and market-creating innovation play in the development of economies in emerging markets. The course will explore the potential economic development opportunities brought forward by market-creation innovations in emerging markets and will seek to understand the unique challenges and opportunities of innovation which face entrepreneurs in emerging markets.
