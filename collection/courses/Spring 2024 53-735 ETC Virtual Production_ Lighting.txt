<start course description for 53-735 ETC Virtual Production: Lighting>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Film and television production is moving towards a combination of physical sets and actors combined with actors and costumes to realize modern storytelling. This class begins by hands-on workshops teaching the basics of traditional cinematic lighting then moves into using those skills and adapting them with virtual tools such as Maya and Unreal 5.
