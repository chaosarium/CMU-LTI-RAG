<start course description for 51-824 Basic 3D Prototyping>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: A half-semester laboratory mini-course introducing a range of materials, methods, and workshop techniques by which designers prototype designs in three dimensions. Basic competence in 3D Lab (shop) techniques is established by bringing to realization a series of simple artifacts. Studio and model shop tools are required. Lab fee. Instructor permission required for non-Design grad students.
</end course description for 51-824 Basic 3D Prototyping>
