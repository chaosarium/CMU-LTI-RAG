<start course description for 60-399 Critical Studies Independent Study>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: A tutorial course in which an Art student works individually on a self-generated project under the supervision of a School of Art faculty member.  Prior to enrolling in Independent Study, the student must complete an "Independent Study Proposal" form (available in the bins on the 3rd floor of CFA) which is signed by the faculty member and the Assistant Head of the School of Art. Prerequisite: Art junior or senior status, or by instructor permission.
</end course description for 60-399 Critical Studies Independent Study>
