<start course description for 24-104 Maker Series: Intro to Modern Making>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: The course familiarizes students with the safe operation of fabrication tools, including 3D printer, laser cutter, hand tools and power tools through structured activities.  Included as preparation for modern making, a significant portion of the course is dedicated to learning the use of SolidWorks 3D CAD software. The acquisition of these skills culminates in the development and fabrication of a prototype solution to a real-world problem.
</end course description for 24-104 Maker Series: Intro to Modern Making>
