Course Description: This recurring course meets regularly throughout the fall and spring semester, giving students exposure to a wide array of industry leaders in innovation and product development. Students can expect expert guests to offer a combination of talks, activities, advice, hands-on skill sessions, and general Q&A; sessions to share their expertise  The seminar schedule for each semester is frequently updated and, depending on the guest, may require minor preparation for the session. This is a required course in the MIIPS degree program.
