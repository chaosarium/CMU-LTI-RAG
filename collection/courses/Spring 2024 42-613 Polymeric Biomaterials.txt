<start course description for 42-613 Polymeric Biomaterials>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course will cover aspects of polymeric biomaterials in medicine from molecular principles to device scale design and fabrication. Topics include the chemistry, characterization, and processing of synthetic polymeric materials; cell-biomaterials interactions including interfacial phenomena, tissue responses, and biodegradation mechanisms; aspects of polymeric micro-systems design and fabrication for applications in medical devices. Recent advances in these topics will also be discussed.
</end course description for 42-613 Polymeric Biomaterials>
