Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: Build on what you learned in Flame 1. Continue to develop hand and glass control, learn to effectively multitask, and develop a greater confidence with a variety of flameworking techniques. You'll discover new skills such as working with Borosilicate tubing and color application while focusing on personal projects. Both group and one-on-one demonstrations will be provided as students work independently. Flame 1, or 24 hours of flameworking experience and instructor permission required. Registration can only be done on or after your scheduled registration day. Please email Stefanie McGowan (stefanim@andrew.cmu.edu) for more information and to register. Registration is first come, first served. Course fee is $190. Not eligible for PCHE Cross Registration. Class is held at Pittsburgh Glass Center. Courses at the Pittsburgh Glass Center will run from February 6th-April 7th.
