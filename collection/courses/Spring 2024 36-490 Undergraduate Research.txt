<start course description for 36-490 Undergraduate Research>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course is designed to give undergraduate students experience using statistics in real research problems. Small groups of students will be matched with clients and do supervised research for a semester.  Students will gain skills in approaching a research problem, critical thinking, statistical analysis, scientific writing, and conveying and defending their results to an audience.
</end course description for 36-490 Undergraduate Research>
