Course Description: Professional service is important in career development because it creates opportunities to use skills and knowledge, develop leadership abilities, develop professional networks, and to learn the importance of community involvement and social values in business practice.  Students complete a variety of activities with these objectives over a period of up to four semesters to satisfy this course requirement.
