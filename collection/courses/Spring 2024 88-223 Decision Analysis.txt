<start course description for 88-223 Decision Analysis>
Semester: Spring 2024 (aka S24)
Course Description: This course offers practical guidance about how to make better decisions and teaches students how to use modeling to do decision analysis.  We analyze decisions involving uncertainty, risk, and time delay. In addition to methods of decision analysis, the course will also emphasize sensitivity analysis and communication of recommendations.
