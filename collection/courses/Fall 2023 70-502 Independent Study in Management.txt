<start course description for 70-502 Independent Study in Management>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: Students with a special interest in Management/Production not covered by a formal Business course may develop an Independent Study Course in that area.  Readings and work to be completed are by agreement between the student and an individual faculty member.  Enrollment by permission of the BA program. This course uses course material that requires students to pay an additional course fee. The course material is secured by the Tepper School because it is otherwise unavailable and directly provided to each student.
</end course description for 70-502 Independent Study in Management>
