<start course description for 54-785 Colloquium>
Semester: Fall 2023 (aka F23)
Course Description: The two-semester Colloquium sequence recognizes the different backgrounds and trajectories of its students. Its primary goal is to provide a space for students from different options to come together and build relationships through participation in a range of artistic and analytic exercises, thus complementing future production work and interactions. Students have the ability to shape the curriculum based on their individual cohort's needs.
