Spring Carnival 2024 Event Reunion Beer Garden

Title: Reunion Beer Garden
Date: April 13, 2024
Time: 2:00 PM-4:00 PM ET

Meet local Carnegie Mellon alumni brewmasters who will be serving samples of their craft beers. The first 300 attendees will receive a commemorative pint glass. Free samples from all our brewers, and one pint pour will be provided. Note: Registration required. No event fee. This event is open to Reunion celebrants and their guests.
