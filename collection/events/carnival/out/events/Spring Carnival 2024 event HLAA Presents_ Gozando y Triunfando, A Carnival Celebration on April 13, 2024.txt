Spring Carnival 2024 Event HLAA Presents: Gozando y Triunfando, A Carnival Celebration

Title: HLAA Presents: Gozando y Triunfando, A Carnival Celebration
Date: April 13, 2024
Time: 9:00 AM-12:00 PM ET

Join us to celebrate the establishment of CMU's Hispanic Latino Alumni Association (HLAA) as we look to add representation, and voice to CMU's Hispanic & Latino community. During this event we will discuss HLAAs establishment, vision and accomplishments in FY23. We will learn about the dynamism and drive of our current students, collect alumni feedback and needs as we look to solidify our place amongst the greater CMU community. Note: Registration required. Walk-ins are welcome as space permits. No event fee. This event is open to the HLAA community and their guests.
