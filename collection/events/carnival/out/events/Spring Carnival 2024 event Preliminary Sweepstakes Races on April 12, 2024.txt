Title: Preliminary Sweepstakes Races

Come watch the CMU tradition where teams compete in an exciting sport best described as a mix between a relay race and bobsled! Can't make it back to campus? We'll livestream the races through cmuTV. This event is open to the entire CMU community and their guests.
